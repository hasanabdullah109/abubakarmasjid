MADRASA ABU BAKAR

    Our Masjid Managment decided that thay should start a Madrasa in Masjid. So there is well managed Madrasa with fully aducated and disciplined teachers. Boys and Girls can learn Quran, Tafseer, Tarjuma, Hadees and other Islamic things. Aged ledies and gentelmen can also come to Madrasa.
    Here some types of classes that are provided by our Madrasa.

1. FULL TIME HIFZ CLASSES

    These Classes are for the students who give their full day for Hifz e Quran. Thay start their study after FAJR PRAYER and carries on till ESHA PRAYER. The girls can go to their home after ASR PRAYER.

2. PART TIME HIFZ CLASSES

    This is for the students who can't give their full day and thay want to HIFZ QURAN with their school. Thay can come after ZUHR PRAYER or any time thay want for one or two hours.

3. NAZRA CLASSES

    The students who want to Read QURAN NAZRA can come for an hour or half hour any time thay want. The aged women and men can also come any time.

4. TARJUMA AND TAFSEER CLASSES

    The students who want to Read QURAN with TARJUMA and TAFSEER can come for an hour or half hour any time thay want. The aged women and men can also come any time.
